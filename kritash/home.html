Our purpose of existence is to enhance the social consciousness of society.

Kritash was founded in 2017 by Abhishek Kumar Gupta (mechanical undergraduate), as the Social Welfare Body of IIT Jammu. That brought up a group of students together to contribute towards the betterment of society with a focus of teaching underprivileged children. We share a common dream-” To create awareness and work in empowering children by providing them fundamental knowledge to sustain themselves in near future.”
